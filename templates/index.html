Where Should We Build Our Brewery?

Brew Buds Marketing Inc. was contracted to grow the "Ale-mpire" of the "Holy-Ale" Brewing Company.

We researched several criteria to help you determine the best locations to open your next brewery: income, population, age, current breweries, and The Starbucks Standard.

And the winners are:

  1. Washington, D.C.
  2. Brooklyn, NY
  3. Bay Area, CA
  4. Cambridge, MA
  5. Houston, TX
